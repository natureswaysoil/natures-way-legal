Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 30, 2025

Important: This privacy policy covers our automated video content creation and social media posting services, including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest integrations.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address when you create an account
  • Social media account information when you connect platforms
  • Basic profile information from connected social media accounts

Social Media Data

  • Profile information (username, display name) for content posting
  • Account verification status to ensure proper authorization
  • Posting permissions and scope access as granted by you

Usage Data

  • Content posting history and performance metrics
  • Website usage analytics and interaction data
  • Error logs and system performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Automated Content Creation: Generate educational videos about natural health and gardening
  • Social Media Posting: Post content to your connected accounts (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest)
  • Account Management: Maintain your user account and preferences
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Communication: Send important updates about our services

3. Social Media Platform Integration

TikTok Integration

  • We use TikTok's Content Posting API to upload educational videos
  • We access basic user information for account verification
  • Videos focus on natural health education and gardening tips
  • Posted content complies with TikTok's community guidelines

Other Platforms

  • Similar integration principles apply to Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest
  • We only post educational content related to natural health and gardening
  • All content is generated automatically and reviewed for compliance

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Social Media Platforms: When posting content to your connected accounts
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or acquisition

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission and storage
  • Secure API connections with social media platforms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel only

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect social media accounts at any time
  • Opt-out: Stop automated posting services

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • As long as your account is active
  • For up to 30 days after account deletion for backup purposes
  • Longer if required by law or for legitimate business purposes

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your privacy rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Cookies and Tracking

  • Improve our services and user experience
